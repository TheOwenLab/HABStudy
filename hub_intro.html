Your HAB Study Hub ▼
Thank you for contributing to our research! This interactive dashboard tracks your progress across 9 study components. You can complete these modules in any order you like, and your progress is saved automatically.
Mobile Warning: If you are on a mobile phone, please re-open the study link using a laptop, desktop computer, or tablet.
  • 1
    Explore Modules: Move your mouse over the different areas of the brain graphic below to view descriptions and estimated completion times.
  • 2
    Begin an Activity: Click on any illuminated brain piece to launch that specific module. Grayed-out modules are waiting to be started, while fully bright modules are complete.
  • 3
    Review Your Data: After finishing your first module, the "View Results" button at the bottom will unlock, allowing you to view your personalized summary performance metrics.
