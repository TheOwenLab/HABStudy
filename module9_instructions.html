Module 9: Additional Information ▼
The brain games you completed evaluate specific cognitive domains, but real-world data is heavily influenced by your environment, your device, and your daily habits. This optional module gives you an open canvas to share the context behind your scores.
  • 1
    Share What is Relevant: You do not need to write an essay for every field. Provide details only for the questions that apply to your experiences today or your general background.
  • 2
    Feel Free to Skip: If a specific section does not apply to you (for example, if you had absolutely no environmental distractions), simply leave the text area blank.
  • 3
    No Strict Formatting: Do not worry about spelling or perfect phrasing. Bullet points or rough sentences are perfect—our team of scientists read these manually to spot situational context!
