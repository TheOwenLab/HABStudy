🔒 Privacy & Anonymity ▼
To ensure your research data remains entirely anonymous, please do not include any personally identifying details in your open-text entries. Your responses will be scrubbed for anything that appears like identifying information by our team of scientists, and anything you write will be kept separate from your contact profile. Regardless, please follow these guidelines:
  • ✕
    Direct Identifiers: Do not write your name, email address, phone number, or the names of family members or clinicians.
  • ✕
    Specific Locations: Avoid using your exact home address, specific neighborhood names, or regional postal codes.
  • ✕
    Named Institutions: Do not explicitly name companies, specific clinics, or hospitals. Instead, use generic descriptions (e.g., write "my employer" or "the local clinic").
